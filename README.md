# Project Atlas

Project Atlas is an Autonomous Marketing Operating System for small businesses.

Atlas is not a campaign builder or a copywriting tool. It is an AI marketing employee that builds a **Digital Twin** of every business it serves — a living model of the company's catalog, brand, audience, and history — and uses that model to proactively surface recommendations, prepare campaigns, and learn over time.

At the center of every company is a **Business Brain**: a structured knowledge base that Atlas continuously updates as it observes the business. The Business Brain powers the Opportunity Engine and Decision Engine, which determine what marketing should happen next — before the user ever has to ask.

Atlas does not wait for prompts. It identifies opportunities, prepares everything for approval, and executes once a human says yes.

Working product name: MarketingOS
Internal project name: Project Atlas
